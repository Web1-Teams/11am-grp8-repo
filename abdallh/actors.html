Brad pitt

Brad Pitt is an acclaimed American actor and producer, born on December 18, 1963.
Known for his versatility, he has starred in iconic films such as Fight Club,
Ocean's Eleven, and Once Upon a Time in Hollywood.
Pitt has won two Academy Awards: one as a producer for 12 Years
a Slave and another as Best Supporting Actor for Once Upon a Time in Hollywood.
Beyond acting, he is also recognized for his humanitarian efforts and philanthropy.
Pitt has been a prominent figure in campaigns for
luxury brands like Chanel and Brioni
