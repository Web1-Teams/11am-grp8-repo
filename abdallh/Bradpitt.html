| Bio

Early Life and Background:

  • ♦ Birth and upbringing:
    • ♦ Born December 18, 1963, in Shawnee, Oklahoma; raised in Springfield, Missouri.
    • ♦ Parents: Jane Etta Pitt (school counselor) and William Alvin "Bill" Pitt (truck company manager).
  • ♦ Education:
    • ♦ Attended Kickapoo High School: involved in sports, debating, student government, and school musicals.
    • ♦ Studied journalism at the University of Missouri but left two credits short of graduation to move to California.
  • ♦ Early jobs before acting:
    • ♦ Worked as a driver for strippers, moved refrigerators, and dressed as a chicken for El Pollo Loco.

Early Career and Breakthrough:

  • ♦ Early acting roles:
    • ♦ Started on the soap opera Another World and the recurring role of Randy on Dallas.
    • ♦ Gained attention with Thelma & Louise (1991) as a charming criminal.
  • ♦ Early career struggles:
    • ♦ Starred in poorly received films like Johnny Suede (1991) and Cool World (1992).
  • ♦ Breakthrough success:
    • ♦ A River Runs Through It (1992) established him as a serious actor.
    • ♦ Memorable roles in True Romance (1993), Interview with the Vampire (1994), and Legends of the Fall (1994).
    • ♦ Acclaimed performance in 12 Monkeys (1995), winning a Golden Globe.

Established Career and Personal Life:

  • ♦ Major roles:
    • ♦ Starred in Se7en (1995), Troy (2004), Mr. & Mrs. Smith (2005), Inglourious Basterds (2009), and Moneyball (2011).
    • ♦ Received Best Actor Oscar nominations for The Curious Case of Benjamin Button (2008) and Moneyball (2011).
    • ♦ Recent work includes Fury (2014).

  • ♦ Personal life:

    • ♦ Married Jennifer Aniston in 2000 but left her for Angelina Jolie in 2005.
    • ♦ Has six children with Jolie: three adopted and three biological.
